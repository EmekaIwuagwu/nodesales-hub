# Privacy Policy for Kortana Wallet

*Last Updated: March 2026*

## 1. Overview
Kortana Wallet (the "Extension") is a decentralized, non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet and interface for the Kortana blockchain. We are committed to protecting user privacy through a **Privacy-by-Design** architecture.

## 2. No Data Collection
Kortana Wallet operates as a **client-side local enclave**. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to our servers.
*   **No Accounts**: We do not require usernames, real names, or email addresses to use the wallet.
*   **No Tracking**: We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics scripts to monitor user behavior.
*   **No IP Logging**: We do not log IP addresses or geographic locations.

## 3. Local Data Sovereignty
All sensitive data required to operate the wallet—including your private keys, encrypted mnemonic phrases, and enclave configurations—is stored exclusively on your local device using secure browser storage.
*   **Encryption**: Local state is encrypted using a user-provided password.
*   **Custody**: You are in sole control of your assets. We have no access to your private keys and cannot recover them if lost.

## 4. Blockchain Interactions
The Extension interacts with the Kortana blockchain via public RPC endpoints (e.g., `zeus-rpc.mainnet.kortana.xyz`). These interactions are necessary to broadcast transactions, fetch balances, and synchronize with the Enclave's global state. 

## 5. Third-Party Services
If you choose to use third-party dApps or bridges connected via the Extension, your interaction is governed by the privacy policies of those specific services.

## 6. Security
Kortana Wallet uses ZK-verified (Zero-Knowledge) compliance protocols to ensure privacy while allowing for audited interactions. No cryptographic secrets are ever exposed during these computations.
