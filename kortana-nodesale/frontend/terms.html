2. Node License Specifications

A Kortana Node License grants the holder the exclusive right to earn programmatic network rewards distributed native in DNR via Smart Contract execution. Licenses are represented exactly as cryptographic Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) deployed on the Kortana Blockchain.

  • Reward yields are mathematically calculated based on the Tier of the purchased License.
  • DNR allocations are distributed globally every 24 Hours explicitly to the holding wallet.
  • Holding multiple Licenses compounds daily rewards algorithmically.

3. Payment and Transfer

Purchases must be remitted strictly via USDT on the supported network protocols specified on the official Kortana ecosystem frontend. Once payment is mathematically confirmed on the block explorer, the corresponding NFT will be minted directly to your specified Enclave wallet.

Because Blockchain ledgers are securely immutable, refunds are strictly not provided under any circumstance once the NFT has been broadcasted to the Purchaser's wallet.

4. Transferability and Resale

Node Licenses are fully sovereign and completely transferable cryptographic assets. You may securely sell, trade, or transfer your Node License to any other Kortana Network wallet holder without seeking permission. Upon transfer, all future automated DNR airdrops are immediately re-routed to the new receiving wallet.
