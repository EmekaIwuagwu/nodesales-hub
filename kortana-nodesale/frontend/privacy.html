Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

The Kortana Foundation strictly respects user privacy. We only collect the minimal structural data necessary to execute Node License purchases legally and securely:

  • Public Wallet Addresses: Cryptographic keys used to securely mint and deliver your NFTs on-chain.
  • Email Addresses: Utilized exclusively for structural communication, receipt dispatch, and critical Mainnet deployment notices.
  • Transaction Hashes: Used strictly to map USDT verifications mathematically to License issuances.

2. How We Route Your Data

Your Kortana Wallet address is structurally integrated into the Kortana License Smart Contract public ledger. Because block explorers are fundamentally transparent by nature, your ownership of the Node NFT and daily DNR reward yields are mathematically visible on the Native Explorer.

Your Email address is strictly secured on our private administrative vaults and is never sold, packaged, or distributed to generic third-party advertisers.

3. Decentralization Protocol

We do NOT track IP Addresses, geographic metadata, or personal identities required by Web2 entities. The Node Sale interacts directly with your decentralized EVM Environment without requesting KYC tracking frameworks, preserving Web3 sovereign isolation.

4. Contact the Foundation

If you have any explicit questions regarding how we process the minimal data payloads utilized during your Node purchase, securely contact our global network administrators at privacy@kortana.network.
