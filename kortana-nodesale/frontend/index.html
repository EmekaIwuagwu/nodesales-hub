Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical knowledge to earn rewards? +
No. Zero technical knowledge required. Once you hold the License NFT in your Kortana wallet, DNR rewards flow automatically every 36 minutes. You do nothing.
How do I receive my DNR rewards? +
Rewards are sent automatically to the Kortana wallet address you provide at purchase. Add Kortana Mainnet to MetaMask (Chain ID: 9002) to see your growing DNR balance.
Can I sell my node license? +
Yes. Your license is an NFT and fully transferable. You can sell it to anyone at any time. Rewards will automatically go to the new owner.
What is DNR? +
DNR (DINAR) is the native token of Kortana Blockchain. It powers all transactions, staking, and governance on the network. It is not an ERC20 token — it is native to Kortana like ETH is native to Ethereum.
